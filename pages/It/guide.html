Analysis

   

The main topic of my project is Forough Farrokhzad. The chosen items are all relevant in order to tell the facts about her story. The purpose of this conceptual map is to represent the extracted data, all in natural language. This step represents data as extracted from the choosen 10 items and also the relationips between them. All greean nodes in the graph represent the items , while blue nodes represent important events, places and people related to the main subject.

    Here, the life of the renowned Iranian poet, Forugh Farrokhzad, is divided into three main stages. Each stage encompasses different periods of the artist's life: childhood and adolescence, the period of marriage and poetry, and the phase from divorce to death.
