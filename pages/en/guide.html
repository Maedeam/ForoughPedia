User Guide


  • User Guide

    The ForoughPedia can be explored with the help of moving along 3 different itineraries, each divided into 3 stages. In every stages, users can read about 3 aspect of Forough: her life, work and mindset, also and stage for "After Death" informations.
    The menu bar allows users to access all pages, in every part of the ForoughPedia, including the ability to toggle between 3 different languages: English, Italian, and Persian (Farsi).

  • Stage 1

    During this period of Forugh's life, we will delve into her personal life and experiences during childhood and adolescence. The "Life" section will provide a detailed account of the events and experiences of this stage. In "Works," we will examine the creations born out of this period, including her poems and writings. Additionally, "Ideology" will elucidate Forugh's mindset and ideals during this phase
