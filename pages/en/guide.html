User Guide


  • User Guide

    The ForoughPedia can be explored with the help of moving along 3 different itineraries, each divided into 3 Stages. In every stages, users can read about 3 aspect of Forough: her life, work and mindset, also and stage for "After Death" informations.
    The menu bar allows users to access all pages, in every part of the ForoughPedia,
    including the ability to toggle between 3 different languages: English, Italian, and Persian (Farsi).
    It is recommended to explore this site according to the order of the menu bar.

  • Stage 1

    During this period of Forugh's life,
    we will delve into her personal life and experiences during childhood and adolescence.

    The "Life" section will provide a detailed account of the events and experiences of this stage.
    In "Works", we will examine the creations
    born out of this period,
    including her poems and writings.

    Additionally, "Ideology" will elucidate Forugh's mindset and ideals during this phase.

  • Stage 2

    This stage explores Forugh's life during marriage,
    residing in different cities,
    and creating poetic works.

    The "Life" section will detail her personal life during this period.
    "Works" will analyze her poetic and prose creations during this phase.

    Moreover, "Ideology" will elaborate on her mindset and ideals during this stage.

  • Stage 3

    This phase covers Forugh's life post-divorce,
    her entry into the realm of cinema and documentary filmmaking,
    up to her final years.

    "Life" will intricately discuss her personal life during this period.

    In "Works," we will explore her artistic contributions in this stage,

    and "Ideology" will shed light on her thoughts and ideals during this time.

  • After Death

    In this stage, we examine Forugh's life and artistic legacy after her passing.

    This section thoroughly analyzes her literary and artistic legacy,
    her impact on society, and her posthumous recognition.
