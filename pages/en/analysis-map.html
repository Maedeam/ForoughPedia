Analysis

   

Forough's works have been translated into numerous languages worldwide, and many analyses and biographies have been written about her.
Forough was among the poets whose personal lives always attracted attention, alongside reading her works, there's often a look into her life and biography. Therefore, most translations include lengthy biographies and analyses.
The exhaustive search eventually yielded scattered results from many countries around the world. The primary factor in searching for translated works was the official status of the book and the presence of an ISBN. Although there are many unofficial sources, this list has been limited to works that are firstly official, and secondly available in printed book form. Many of these translations had original publication website links, while for many others, only links to the Goodreads website were available. However, many books existed in countries such as Afghanistan, India, and Tajikistan that did not have ISBNs or links to any publishers. Therefore, they were excluded from the list. It is evident that this list could be more extensive, and there is no claim to its completeness
The comple table of books are available in the Translations Page of the Foroughpedia website.
In that page, Each book has its own properties like name and translator or publication name and ISBN even the direct links of the publication website, if available, and also in most cases, the link of Goodreads site.

   

Geo Frequency


After compiling the list of Forough's translated works, two analyses were prepared and visualized.
The first analysis refers to the countries in which the translation is located.
Clicking on a continent zooms in on that continent to better view the countries in it.
Each country displays a list of available book names that are clickable, and by clicking on that,
you can to the Translations Page of Foroughpedia website.







   

Heat Map


In the second analysis, the question
"How many translations are there in each country?"
is addressed. With a quick glance at this geographical map, one can see that Turkey, the neighboring country, has the highest number of official translations of Forough's works, followed by the United States and then significantly by other countries
