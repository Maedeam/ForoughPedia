About the Project




The Forough Pedia Project, developed as the thesis within DHDK Master's Degree at Alma Mater Studiorum - Università di Bologna, a.y. 2023/2024. This project stands as a knowledge website dedicated to the life and works of Forough Farrokhzad, the greatest contemporary female poet of Iran. Supervised by Professor Paola Italia, with co-supervision by Professor Francesca Tomasi, the project offers a user-friendly trilingual interface, ensuring accessibility for diverse audiences.


In this project, besides examining different stages of Forough's life from different perspectives of life, works and mindset, the text analysis and data visualization tools have been used to explore new dimensions of Forough's poetry. Thematic analysis categorizes Forough's writings based on recurring themes and provides the interactive charts for users. The dataset, gathered in CSV format through digital text analysis, supports these analyses and is presented visually. A significant portion of the project focuses on creating an engaging website.


Undoubtedly, Foroughpedia has the potential for expansion and enhancement to serve as an inspirational model for showcasing the lives and works of other influential figures worldwide. This endeavor aspires to be a catalyst for further exploration and understanding, fostering an enthusiasm for knowledge-sharing and cultural exchange.

In this section, the important details about the datasets are provided, including their descriptions, the planning decisions made, and insights into the methods and tools utilized during the compilation process. But why this section is titled Capta instead of Data? To emphasize a key idea and concept that significantly impacted the work. This concept is commonly used in Digital Humanities, as defined by Johanna Drucker: https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johanna_Drucker ""Capta is “taken” actively while data is assumed to be a “given” able to be recorded and observed. From this distinction, a world of differences arises. Humanistic inquiry acknowledges the situated, partial, and constitutive character of knowledge production, the recognition that knowledge is constructed, taken, not simply given as a natural representation of pre-existing fact. (Drucker, 2011)"" To visually show something, there is a need to turn it into numbers and tables. This second model is E/R Model, a translation of conceptual model that is displaying data and theire relationships from the previous conceptual map in a more abstracted layer that consider the entities, relationships, and attributes. Here we represent people, dates, subjects, places etc. as entities and the connections between them as relationships in a same graphical representation.

The main topic of my project is Forough Farrokhzad. The chosen items are all relevant in order to tell the facts about her story. The purpose of this conceptual map is to represent the extracted data, all in natural language. This step represents data as extracted from the choosen 10 items and also the relationips between them. All greean nodes in the graph represent the items , while blue nodes represent important events, places and people related to the main subject.
