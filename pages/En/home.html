About the Project




The Forough Pedia Project, developed as the thesis within DHDK Master's Degree at Alma Mater Studiorum - Universit√† di Bologna, a.y. 2022/2023. This project stands as a knowledge website dedicated to the life and works of Forough Farrokhzad, the greatest contemporary female poet of Iran. Supervised by Professor Paola Italia, with co-supervision by Professor Francesca Tomasi, the project offers a user-friendly trilingual interface, ensuring accessibility for diverse audiences.


In this project, besides examining different stages of Forough's life from different perspectives of life, works and mindset, the text analysis and data visualization tools have been used to explore new dimensions of Forough's poetry. Thematic analysis categorizes Forough's writings based on recurring themes and provides the interactive charts for users. The dataset, gathered in CSV format through digital text analysis, supports these analyses and is presented visually. A significant portion of the project focuses on creating an engaging website.


Undoubtedly, Foroughpedia has the potential for expansion and enhancement to serve as an inspirational model for showcasing the lives and works of other influential figures worldwide. This endeavor aspires to be a catalyst for further exploration and understanding, fostering an enthusiasm for knowledge-sharing and cultural exchange.
